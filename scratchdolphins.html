About Dolphins

Dolphins are highly intelligent marine mammals and are part of the family of toothed whales that includes orcas and pilot whales.

Types of Dolphins

There are over 40 species of dolphins. The most common and well-known is the bottlenose dolphin.

  1. types
    • Amazon river dolphin
    • Bottlenose dolphin
    • Irrawaddy dolphin
    • Oceanic dolphins
    • Spinner dolphin
    • Orinoco river dolphin
  2. Life span
    • male dolphins live about 40 years
    • female dolphines can live up to 60 years

Conservation Efforts

Conservation efforts for dolphins include

  1. protection from fishing nets
  2. ensuring clean marine habitats
  3. research on dolphin populations
